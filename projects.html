+ - codeInn_pixels

codeInn_pixels.com was my first personal project, developed to help other self-learning students like me to begin their journey in front-end development, immersing them into a short experience throughout its basics.

Originally a practise exercise, I eventually grew very attached to it, ultimately deciding to carry on improving it as I too carried on learning.
This project has therefore been under development for a long time, so much so that this too would become part of its essence:
learning front-end development together.
